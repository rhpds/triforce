== The Takeaway

Most enterprise AI does not need a GPU. With the right model selection, the right optimizations, and intelligent routing, Intel Xeon 6 CPUs handle 80% or more of real-world inference at zero incremental cost.

The 20% that genuinely needs GPU acceleration -- complex reasoning, frontier models, latency-critical generation -- routes to Intel Gaudi. The rest stays on the CPUs already in your data center.

That is not a pitch. You measured it.
